Deterministic Finite Automata

  • DFA are tuples defined as (Q, Σ, δ, q0, F)
  • Q is the finite set of states
  • Σ is the alphabet
  • δ : :Q ⋅ Σ → Q are the set of transition funcions
  • q0 ∈ Q is the inital state
  • F ∈ Q is the final states

Extended Transition function

  • $ : Q x σ^* -> Q $
  • $ (q_0, xa) = (((q, x)), a) $

Languages of DPA A = (Q, (sigma), (delta), q0, F)

  • $ L(A) - {w ^* : (q0, w) (in) F} $
